# Enterprise Leave Policy Document

## Section 1: Annual Leave
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
Leave must be approved by the reporting manager.

## Section 2: Sick Leave
Employees can take up to 10 days of sick leave per year.
A medical certificate is required for absences longer than 2 days.

## Section 3: Casual Leave
Employees are eligible for 5 days of casual leave annually for personal matters.

## Section 4: Maternity Leave
Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.
This applies to all full-time employees after probation.

## Section 5: Paternity Leave
Male employees are entitled to 5 days of paternity leave.

## Section 6: Leave Without Pay
If leave balances are exhausted, employees may apply for unpaid leave subject to manager approval.

## Section 7: Carry Forward Policy
Unused annual leave can be carried forward up to 10 days to next year.

## Section 8: Leave Application Process
All leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 3 days in advance.
