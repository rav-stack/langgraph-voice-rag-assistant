VPN TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE

1. Check Internet Connection:
Ensure you have a stable internet connection before connecting to VPN.

2. Restart VPN Client:
Close and reopen the VPN application. Try reconnecting.

3. Verify Credentials:
Make sure your username and password are correct.

4. Switch Network:
Try connecting using a different network (e.g., mobile hotspot).

5. Update VPN Client:
Ensure you are using the latest version of the VPN software.

6. Firewall/Antivirus Check:
Temporarily disable firewall or antivirus to see if they are blocking the connection.

7. Contact IT Support:
If the issue persists, contact IT support with error screenshots and logs.
