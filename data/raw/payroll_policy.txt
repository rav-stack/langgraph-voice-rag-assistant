`PAYROLL POLICY

1. Salary Payment Cycle:
Salaries are processed on the last working day of each month.

2. Payslip Access:
Employees can download their payslips from the HR portal under the "Payroll" section.

3. Tax Deductions:
Income tax and other statutory deductions are calculated as per government regulations.

4. Bonuses:
Performance bonuses are paid annually based on individual and company performance.

5. Reimbursements:
Approved reimbursements (travel, internet, etc.) are processed within 10 working days.

6. Salary Revisions:
Salary revisions are typically conducted once a year during the appraisal cycle.

7. Payroll Queries:
Employees can raise payroll-related queries via the HR support system or email payroll@company.com.
