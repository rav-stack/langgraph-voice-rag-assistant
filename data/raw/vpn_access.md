# VPN Access Guide

## Requesting VPN Access
To request VPN access, submit a ticket through the IT Service Portal under
"Network Access > VPN Request". Include your manager's name for approval.

## Approval Time
VPN requests are typically approved within 1 business day. Requests submitted
after 5 PM are processed the next working day.

## Connecting to the VPN
1. Install the GlobalProtect client from the Software Center.
2. Open the client and enter the portal address: vpn.company-sample.net
3. Log in with your corporate username and password.
4. Approve the multi-factor authentication prompt on your phone.

## Troubleshooting
If the VPN fails to connect, first check your internet connection. If the issue
persists, restart the GlobalProtect client. For "authentication failed" errors,
reset your password and try again.
